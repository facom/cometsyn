
Description of Table Columns
----------------------------

"Object Designation" 
    Name and/or designation of the asteroid or comet 

"Epoch" 
    Epoch of the osculating orbital elements 
    (Modified Julian Date = Julian Date - 2400000.5) 

"Ref." 
    Orbit solution reference: "JPL..." = Jet Propulsion Laboratory, 
    "MPC..." = Minor Planet Center, "IHW..." = International Halley Watch,
    "BM..." = Brian G. Marsden, and "BGMCAT..." = Brian G. Marsden Catalog 

"a (AU)" 
    Semi-major axis (AU) 

"q (AU)" 
    Perihelion distance (AU) 

"e" 
    Eccentricity 

"i (deg)" 
    Inclination (degrees, J2000 ecliptic) 

"W (deg)" 
    Longitude of the ascending node (degrees, J2000 ecliptic) 

"w (deg)" 
    Argument of perihelion (degrees, J2000 ecliptic) 

"M (deg)" 
    Mean anomoly (degrees) 

"TP" 
    Time of perihelion passage (YYYYMMDD.DDDDD) 

"P (yr)" 
    Orbital period (years) 

"Rec.#" 
    DASTCOM record number
